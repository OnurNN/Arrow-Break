# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2025  
**App:** ArrowBreak  
**Developer:** OnurNN

---

## 1. Overview

ArrowBreak ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile puzzle game available on the Apple App Store. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Provide
- **Apple ID / Sign In with Apple:** If you choose to sign in, we receive only your anonymized Apple User Identifier. We never receive your real name or email unless you explicitly share it.

### 2.2 Data Collected Automatically
- **CloudKit (iCloud):** Level progress, completed levels, and difficulty settings are stored in your personal iCloud container. This data is tied to your Apple ID and is never accessible to us.
- **Game Progress (Local):** When not signed in, progress is stored locally on your device using `UserDefaults`. This data never leaves your device.

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect your name, email address, or phone number.
- We do **not** track your location.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase, Mixpanel).
- We do **not** sell your data to any third party.

---

## 3. Advertising

ArrowBreak may display ads powered by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA) for ad personalization, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent on iOS 14+.

- If you opt out of tracking, only non-personalized ads will be shown.
- AdMob's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 4. In-App Purchases

ArrowBreak offers optional in-app purchases (e.g., Remove Ads) processed entirely by Apple. We do not store any payment information.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

ArrowBreak does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no features that require personal data from children.

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

- Local data is stored on-device via `UserDefaults`.
- Cloud data is stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, protected by Apple's security standards.
- We do not operate our own servers.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your data (use **Settings → Delete Account** in the app)
- Opt out of personalized advertising (use iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking)

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
